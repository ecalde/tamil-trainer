# Curriculum roadmap

Long-term target: **roughly 2,000–3,000** high-utility spoken items phased by frequency and communicative function. The MVP ships a **small, high-quality Stage 1** seed (`src/content/seed/curriculum.ts`) that already mixes into short conversational situations.

## Stage 1 (seed implemented)

Focus: **survival conversation** — greetings, politeness, pronouns + deixis, essential verbs and needs, question words, numbers 1–5.

Lessons (see app path):

1. Hello and checking in  
2. Thanks, sorry, please  
3. I, you, we, here, there  
4. Going, coming, wanting, food and water  
5. Question words and numbers 1–5  

## Stage 2 (roadmap)

- Food and ordering  
- Directions and places  
- Time and schedule  
- Home and routine  
- Emotions and states  
- Simple descriptions and requests  
- Light past/future **through fixed phrases** (not abstract grammar drilling)

## Stage 3 (roadmap)

- Social conversation  
- Work and study  
- Travel  
- Health basics  
- Preferences and opinions  
- Mini dialogues and short listening drills  
- Phrase variations

## Stage 4+ (roadmap)

- Expand toward **2k–3k** items with strong coverage of sentence patterns  
- Reinforce common frames (“want/need”, “going/coming”, “can/can’t”, questions, negation)  
- Prepare learners for **simple real-time conversation**

## Design rules

- Prefer **colloquial spoken** Tamil where practical; note register when it matters.  
- Teach **phrases before isolated words** when the phrase is more useful alone.  
- Keep lesson sizes small; combine items that form **mini-dialogues** quickly.  
- Reserve schema fields for **Tamil script writing** and richer audio without breaking imports.
